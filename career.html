Open Positions

Graduate Research Assistant (PhD/MS)

WPI Department of Data Science

Join SoilX to advance drone-borne ground penetrating radar, synthetic data generation, and machine learning for root-zone soil moisture mapping. Research spans signal processing, FDTD simulation, field measurements at the in-situ lab and partner farms, and publication in IEEE venues.

Contact PI

What We Look For

📡

Signal Processing

Background in radar, RF, or electromagnetic wave propagation — or strong interest in learning GPR fundamentals.

🧠

Machine Learning

Experience with supervised learning, feature engineering, or deep learning applied to scientific or sensor data.

🚁

Drone & Hardware

Interest in UAV integration, field instrumentation, and hands-on lab or farm measurement campaigns.

⭐

Motivated Researchers

Self-driven students who collaborate well, communicate clearly, and want to impact precision agriculture and water conservation.

How to Apply

  1. 1
    Review our publications

    Read recent journal and conference work on our publications page to understand current research directions.

  2. 2
    Email the PI with CV and research statement

    Send your curriculum vitae, a brief statement of interest, and relevant experience to the principal investigator.

  3. 3
    Apply to WPI graduate programs

    Complete formal admission through the WPI Data Science or related graduate programs.
